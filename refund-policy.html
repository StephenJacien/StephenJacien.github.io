Refund Policy

This Refund Policy explains when refunds are available for digital products sold by Chosen Enigmas and how to request one.

1. Digital Product Nature

Chosen Enigmas sells digital products only, including downloadable or duplicable Notion templates and related digital resources. No physical goods are shipped, and no subscriptions or recurring services are provided.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be granted under the following conditions:

  • The request is submitted within 7 calendar days of purchase
  • The product was not accessed, duplicated, or substantially used
  • A technical issue prevented access and could not be resolved by support
  • The purchase was made in error and reported promptly

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • The digital product has already been accessed, duplicated, or imported
  • The product does not meet personal preferences or expectations
  • Failure to read the product description before purchase
  • Requests made after the refund eligibility window
  • Misuse, modification, or third-party platform issues (e.g., Notion account problems)

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us using the details below and include:

  • Your purchase email address
  • Date of purchase
  • Product name
  • Reason for the request

Requests can be submitted via our Contact page or by emailing support@chosenenigmas.com.

5. Processing Time

Approved refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days, depending on your payment provider. Once processed, funds will be returned to the original payment method used at checkout.

6. Policy Updates

Chosen Enigmas reserves the right to update or modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will take effect immediately upon publication on this page.
