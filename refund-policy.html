Refund Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2026

Chosen Enigmas sells digital products delivered electronically. Because digital products can be accessed immediately and duplicated, refunds are limited.

Eligible refunds

  • If you experience a verified technical issue that prevents access to the digital product and we are unable to resolve it within a reasonable timeframe.
  • Refund requests must be submitted within 7 calendar days of purchase.

Non-refundable situations

  • Change of mind after access or delivery has been provided
  • Failure to read the product description before purchasing
  • Incompatibility with third-party platforms, accounts, or software
  • Lack of use or dissatisfaction with subjective outcomes

How to request a refund

Contact us via the Contact page and include: (1) purchase email, (2) purchase date, and (3) a description of the access issue.

Chargebacks

Customers agree to contact us before filing a chargeback. Unresolved chargebacks may result in permanent loss of access to the digital product.
