Refund Policy

This Refund Policy explains when refunds are available for digital products sold by Chosen Enigmas and how to request one.

Last updated: January 25, 2026.

1. Overview

Chosen Enigmas sells digital products delivered electronically. Due to the nature of digital goods, all sales are considered final once delivery has occurred, except as required by law.

2. Eligibility

Refunds may be considered only if the digital product was not delivered as described or access instructions were not provided. Requests must be submitted within 7 days of purchase.

3. Non-Eligible Cases

Refunds will not be issued for:

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Lack of usage or implementation
  • Dissatisfaction with results or outcomes
  • Failure to use or understand the template
  • Compatibility issues related to third-party platforms such as Notion

4. Request Process

To request a refund, contact support@chosenenigmas.com with your purchase details. You may also call 445-544-5550. Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method.

5. Processing Time

Approved refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days, depending on your payment provider. Once processed, funds will be returned to the original payment method used at checkout.

6. Policy Updates

Chosen Enigmas reserves the right to update or modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will take effect immediately upon publication on this page.

7. Finality

By completing a purchase, customers acknowledge that they are purchasing a digital product and agree to this refund policy.
